Joao Lizárraga

I am pursuing a PhD in Physics at the University of Campinas (UNICAMP) in Brazil. My advisor is Marcus de Aguiar, and we work in the Department of Condensed Matter Physics.

My research focuses on systems of interacting individuals that exhibit spatiotemporal activity, most of which are inspired by nature. Currently, I am investigating phenomena related to synchronization and (much more recently) collective motion.

I enjoy finding simple solutions to complex problems, and while my work is primarily theoretical, I rely heavily on numerical simulations.
